Game Rules:

First you have to bat. It means you have to click the buttons in the batting section then the number you clicked will be added. The added number is your score. You can click until the opponent takes the same number as you. If the opponent and you take the same number, you will get out. Then next you have to ball, it's the opponent's chance to bat. If the opponent takes more score than you, you will lose the game. If you get the same number as the opponent and get out before the opponent scores your score, you will win.

Example: your score is 35. Opponent scores: 36 or more. You lose. If you take the same number as the opponent before the opponent takes your score, you win.
